Pizza

pizza, dish of Italian origin consisting of a flattened disk of bread dough topped with some combination of olive oil, oregano, tomato, olives, mozzarella or other cheese, and many other ingredients, baked quickly—usually, in a commercial setting, using a wood-fired oven heated to a very high temperature—and served hot.

There are many types of pizza which include:

  • Margherita Pizza

Margherita pizza is a typical Neapolitan pizza, named after Queen Margherita. It consists of crushed peeled tomatoes, mozzarella and fresh basils on a flat bread.




  • Hawaiian Pizza

Hawaiian Pizza (which is a normal pizza with pineapples that originated in Canada), caused such a controversery between people because of the pineapples on it.



  • Supreme pizza

This vegetarian pizza will delight vegetarians and carnivores alike. It's fresh and full of flavor, featuring cherry tomatoes, artichoke, bell pepper, olives, red onion and some hidden (and optional) baby spinach. You'll find a base of rich tomato sauce and golden, bubbling mozzarella underneath, of course.




There are many types of pizza some with veggies, fruit, meat, seafood......etc.
Every Pizza is delicious and unique in it's own way.
